What can 365 days do?

I came to college on 7-nov-2022 we had ten days till classes start on 16-nov. Out of enthusiasm I wanted to learn python. To learn that I took one of my previous school seniors next wave account I was allotted a room in 4th floor in meenachil hotel in iiit kottayam. At that time wifi is not available in hostel. and phones didn’t get any signals in our hostel. One day I got to know that in ground floor in one room wifi is coming and luckily it was my friends room. There started my journey of python. I that room only in one place wifi works. I used to sit there for the whole day with worst wifi speed and learned python. At that time I don’t even know how to use an IDE I used to run a different file other than the required , with the help of my cousin and some youtube videos I figured it out. In that 10 days I have learnt python upto oops. By then my college started and hostel had new wifi for entire hostel .
